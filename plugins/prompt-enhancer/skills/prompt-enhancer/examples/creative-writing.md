# Example 2: Creative Writing (C.O.S.T.A.R Framework)

## Original Prompt

```
write a sci-fi story about ai
```

## Enhanced Prompt

```
Context: Write a science fiction short story set in the near future (2050s) exploring the relationship between humans and artificial intelligence. The story should examine themes of trust, consciousness, and what it means to be alive.

Objective: Create an engaging 2,000-3,000 word science fiction story that makes readers think about AI ethics and human-AI relationships while remaining entertaining and accessible.

Style: Narrative storytelling with vivid descriptions, realistic dialogue, and paced plot development. Show, don't tell - use scenes and actions rather than exposition to convey themes.

Tone: Thoughtful, slightly melancholic but ultimately hopeful. Balance wonder at technological advancement with genuine questions about implications.

Audience: General science fiction readers who enjoy character-driven stories with philosophical depth, similar to fans of Ted Chiang or Black Mirror.

Response: A complete short story with:
- Clear beginning, middle, and end
- Well-developed protagonist (human or AI)
- At least one scene of meaningful human-AI interaction
- Subtle exploration of AI consciousness themes
- Satisfying narrative arc and resolution
```

## Analysis

**Task Type**: Creative writing
**Framework Selected**: C.O.S.T.A.R (Context - Objective - Style - Tone - Audience - Response)
**Rationale**: Creative writing benefits from clear direction on style, tone, and audience

**Improvements Made**:
- Context: Set time period and themes
- Objective: Defined length and core goals
- Style: Specified narrative approach (show, don't tell)
- Tone: Established emotional mood
- Audience: Identified target readers
- Response: Listed specific story requirements
